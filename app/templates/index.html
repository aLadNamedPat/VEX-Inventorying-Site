Welcome to the Jericho High School Vex Inventorying Site!

This site was created so coachs and students alike could manage and track the distribution of robot parts among a club's different robot teams. To begin, you can create an account as a Coach or Student. If you already have an account, you may just log in.

Coach account functionality:
  • View and manage all VEX parts owned by your club
  • View parts owned by different teams under your organization
  • Manage a wishlist of items that teams want
Student account functionality:
  • View the availibity of the robot parts
  • Create a team inventory to manage what items your team has
  • Submit part requests for your coach to purchase through a wishlist
